[ The journey ]

Three steps, at your pace.

01 Align We get clear on what you truly want, and what it takes to build it. Tap to explore
Discovery Complete We've seen your current tool and heard what your advisors need day to day.
Vision Setting We shape your vision into a clear product direction. It starts with your Vision Brief below.
Scoping & Sizing We define the scope, assess risk & compliance, and size the work honestly, before any number is fixed.

What drives size: scope, data & migration, integrations, compliance, user roles, and SLAs. We share an indicative range early; the firm number comes with the proposal.

02 Plan We agree the terms and design the system before a line of code. Tap to explore
Proposal & Agreements The commercial and legal layer: pricing, a statement of work, a master agreement, and a GDPR data-processing agreement.
Design & Architecture System design, data model, and a security architecture built for regulated finance: encryption, access control, audit trails, and data residency.
03 Build & Run We build it in the open, prove it works, move your data safely, launch, and keep it dependable. Tap to explore
Build Delivered in phases, with a working demo at every milestone, so you steer the product as it takes shape instead of seeing it only at the end.
Testing & Quality Assurance Automated test coverage, security and penetration testing, and user-acceptance testing with your team, so the platform is proven and signed off before it goes anywhere near your clients' data.
Data Migration & Integration A clean, rehearsed move off your legacy tool (mapping, cleansing, validation, cutover), plus the connections your platform needs.
Launch & Rollout Deployment, advisor training, and a controlled go-live so the switch is calm, not disruptive.
Maintenance, Support & Evolution An ongoing, separate package: SLAs, monitoring, security, and a living roadmap. Your team runs it, or we keep it running for you.
