[ Your one piece of homework ]

Before we meet again,
talk it out loud.

The best baseline comes straight from you, thinking out loud. So rather than writing a formal brief, just talk: say everything you want this application to be and do, in your own words. The messier and more honest, the better. That raw version of your vision is exactly what we build our starting point from.

How to do it · about 15 minutes

  1. Open a blank Google Doc.
  2. Turn on Voice typing (Tools → Voice typing), then talk freely about everything you want the application to be and do. Don't stop to tidy it up.
  3. Send the transcript back to us. We'll turn your thinking into a clear product direction together.

Not sure where to start? Let these prompts guide what you talk through:

01

The outcome

A year after launch, what can your advisors and clients do that they can't today?

02

The people

Who lives in this system daily, and what frustrates them most about the tool you use now?

03

The data

What does it hold (net worth, holdings, KYC, documents), and what must move across from today?

04

Must-haves

What's non-negotiable on compliance and security, and what's on the dream list?

05

The connections

What other systems must this talk to? CRM, custodians, market data, identity, e-signature.

06

The one thing

If we nail just one thing, what makes this whole project a success in your eyes?
